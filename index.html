Why a hook?

A hooked handle lets you hang your razor. Convenient spots are your hand towel holder or shower caddy. When hanging upside down, the razor dries at an optimal angle for the blades.

Our hook design provides a better handle for a clean shave. You can adjust your grip as you shave. It's an elegant shape for a frequent activity.
